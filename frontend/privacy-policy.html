Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Lawn Craft ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website (lawncraft.vercel.app), how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit any page on our website, we automatically collect the following technical data to understand how visitors use our site and improve our service:

  • Page URL – the path of the page you visited (e.g. /services)
  • Referrer – the URL of the page that linked you to our site, or "Direct Visit" if none
  • Screen resolution – your device's screen width and height (e.g. 1920x1080)
  • Browser language – your browser's preferred language setting (e.g. en-US)
  • IP address – automatically included in the HTTP request and may be logged by our hosting and analytics infrastructure

This data is sent to an analytics endpoint hosted on Cloudflare Workers. No cookies are set and no persistent identifiers are stored in your browser.

3. Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you submit our contact form or request a quote, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Service of interest
  • Your message

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and to provide you with the lawn care services you have requested.

4. Geolocation

On our Service Area page, you may optionally allow your browser to share your approximate location. This is used only to check whether your property falls within our service area. Your location is not stored on our servers and is not transmitted to any third party.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Understand which pages are most visited and improve our website content
  • Diagnose technical issues and optimise site performance
  • Respond to contact and quote requests
  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Send service-related communications (we will never send unsolicited marketing emails)

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited technical data with the following service providers solely to operate our website:

  • Cloudflare Workers – hosts our analytics endpoint; page URL, referrer, screen resolution, browser language, and IP address are processed on their infrastructure. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel – hosts our static website; standard server access logs (including IP addresses) may be retained by Vercel. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts & Font Awesome – fonts and icons loaded from external CDNs; your browser may send a request to their servers when loading these resources.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions and appointment data are retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the service and meet any applicable legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

Automatically collected analytics data (page views, screen sizes, etc.) is retained for up to 12 months.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. Our API uses JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and HTTPS-enforced transport. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
